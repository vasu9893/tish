üö® Your Issue

Problem: When someone comments on your Instagram posts, no webhook is being sent

Expected: You should receive webhook events in your dashboard when people comment

üîç Common Causes & Solutions

1. Instagram Account Not Connected

Symptom: No webhook subscriptions found

Solution:

  1. Go to your dashboard: https://instantchat.in/dashboard
  2. Connect your Instagram account
  3. Complete the OAuth flow

2. Wrong Webhook URL

Symptom: Webhooks are being sent to wrong endpoint

Solution:

  1. Go to Meta Developer Console
  2. Select your Instagram app
  3. Go to Webhooks section
  4. Update webhook URL to: https://tish-production.up.railway.app/api/webhooks/instagram

3. Missing Webhook Fields

Symptom: Webhooks work for messages but not comments

Solution:

  1. In Meta Developer Console, go to Webhooks
  2. Add these subscription fields:
    comments
    live_comments
    mentions
    message_reactions

4. App Permissions

Symptom: Webhook subscription fails

Solution:

  1. Ensure your app has instagram_business_basic permission
  2. Check that your Instagram account is a Business/Creator account
  3. Verify the account is connected to a Facebook Page

5. Webhook Verification Token

Symptom: Webhook verification fails

Solution:

  1. In Meta Developer Console, set verify token to: instantchat_webhook_2024_secure_token
  2. Make sure the token matches exactly
